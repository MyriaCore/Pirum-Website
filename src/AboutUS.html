Who We Are

Pirum is a company composed of some of the best developers who have been programming for years. We hope to provide exceptional service and only the best products that can be found. Pirum will do everything in their power to make user experience as perfect as possible.

How It All Started

Our story is a long tale of hope and perserverance as five software engineers who wanted to make large amounts of money with little to no effort. Pirum was started nearly 30 years ago as an idea to take values such as the ingenuity of the New Jersey public transportation system and combine it with the the hard work and effort found in the New Jersey public transportation system. Between all five talented developers there is an astounding 3 years of experience, working tiredly to bring the consumers the product they deserve. Our company philosophy has always been work smart, take few risks, and cleanliness is next to godliness. We hope to improve our assets so we as a company can continue to deliver the product the consumers didn't ask for and we haven't made yet and that can only be done by your donations.
